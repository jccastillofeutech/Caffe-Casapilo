Story behind the Classics

Discover the journey behind Caffe Casapilo.




From a Long Century Dream to the Classical Creation

Caffe Casapilo started with a long century ambition since childhood: to create a space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee in a 50s environment. Founded in 2008 by a group of friends who shared a deep passion for coffee craftsmanship, our journey began in a small, unassuming corner in Recto Avenue at Manila. We spent countless hours perfecting our roasts, experimenting with brewing methods, and designing a cafe that felt like a second home.


Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We meticulously source our beans from sustainable farms across the country, ensuring that every cup tells a story of its origin locally. Each batch is roasted with precision and care in-house, unlocking the unique flavors and aromas that make our coffee special and unique at the same time. We believe that great coffee has the power to connect people, inspire creativity, be classical and make positivity in all the years.

Our Mission

To enrich lives by providing an exceptional coffee experience, fostering community, and promoting sustainable practices from bean to cup. Empowered by local culture, we strive to also promote products for all people and Filipinos to thrive in with a taste of coffee.

Our Values

  • Quality - Sourcing the best, roasting with passion, brewing with skill.
  • Community - Creating a welcoming space for all.
  • Sustainability - Ethical sourcing and environmentally conscious operations.
  • Passion - A genuine love for coffee and hospitality.
  • Culture Sharing - Sharing the culture that supports local diversity with the power of coffee
